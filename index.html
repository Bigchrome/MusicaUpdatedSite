What is Musica?
Our Vision

Music in venues and at parties can really suck because you're at the mercy of somebody else's choice. We're here to change that, and give you back control. Users can select and vote on the tracks that are being played.

The Idea
What's great about it

We are building a platform that lets you treat every venue's music system like a personal jukebox for everyone there. Everyone gets to choose their favourite songs, and the best ones will get played first.

Old School Cool
Retro

We've fallen in love with the idea of people picking out their favourite songs like they did on jukeboxes, so we're making this as easy as possible. 21st Century Style.
